HyperReals

A web plataform developed by computer engineering students to help people share books and knowledge.

Why use HypperReals platform?

A strong community is built through the connection between people.

Therefore, our app allows people to connect through their interest in reading and learning.

In addition, we also consider the location factor for connecting people.

This will allow geographically close people to share books and knowledge to strengthen the community in which they live.

Changing a society through education is our greatest wish.

Who is our project for?

Our project is for the community.

A society with transformative capacity is able to grow constantly.

The goal is not the book. The goal is not the paper. The goal is to learn.

Who is our project for?

Our project is for people.

Our goal is to help individuals in a society develop a transformative capacity.

Sharing books and knowledge is the big goal. It is important to remember that good goals elevate the individual and the community in which he is inserted.

Who is our project for?

Our project is for the planet.

Plurality is the law of the earth.

Our project will not help Earth. Our project will help people and people will help the earth.
